🔒 Cream Confidences

Your trust is the most delicate ingredient we handle. At Azure Plate Diary, we follow a strict privacy-first philosophy — zero tracking, zero data harvesting, absolute transparency. This policy outlines how we protect your personal information while you explore our cream chronicles.

📜 Our Pledge: The Cream Covenant

Updated Effective date: 2025-05-05 · Last reviewed: April 2025

Azure Plate Diary operates as a static, privacy-respecting culinary archive. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, cross-site trackers, or any form of persistent user identification. When you visit this website, your browser communicates directly with our server — no scripts harvest your behavior. The only data that could potentially be shared is information you voluntarily provide through our contact form, which is neither stored in any database nor shared with third parties.

✨ The essence of cream transparency: We believe privacy is like a perfect crème anglaise — no hidden lumps, no artificial thickeners. Every interaction stays between you and our kitchen.

🍦 Information Collection — Minimal, Intentional, Ephemeral

What we never collect (zero-tracking policy)

  • No cookies: We do not set any tracking, session, or persistent cookies. No “remember me” functions, no analytics pixels.
  • No IP logging: Our hosting environment does not log individual IP addresses for visitor identification; generic server logs are rotated every 24h and contain no personal data.
  • No behavioral tracking: We don't monitor scroll depth, mouse movements, or page timings.
  • No third-party embeds: We host all fonts, images, and styles locally — no Google Fonts, no YouTube embeds, no external scripts.

Contact form data — what happens to your message

Our Cream Correspondence page includes a contact form that asks for your name, email address, subject, and message. This form is purely front-end simulated for demonstration and security education on the live version. In a production environment, any submission would be transmitted via encrypted email directly to our creamery team. We never store form submissions in any database, CRM, or cloud storage. After responding to your inquiry, all correspondence logs are deleted within 30 days. We do not add your email to any mailing list or newsletter unless you explicitly ask us to (and even then, we only maintain a manual contact list with your consent).

📬 Your Cream Whisper is sacred: When you reach out about clotted cream ratios or velvety béchamel, your email address is used exclusively to reply — never sold, rented, or shared. No automated marketing follows.

🛡️ Data Protection & Security Measures

While we don't store your data, we take the security of any transient communication seriously. Our website is hosted on secure, encrypted servers (HTTPS/TLS 1.3). Any emails sent via our contact mechanism would be delivered over encrypted SMTP channels. We regularly audit dependencies and maintain a minimalist codebase to reduce attack surface. In the unlikely event of a security incident involving your personal message (e.g., unauthorized server access), we will notify affected individuals within 72 hours as required by GDPR/CCPA principles, though such scenario is improbable given our zero-storage architecture.

Children's Privacy: Our content is family-friendly and cream-focused. We do not knowingly collect any information from persons under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has sent us a message, please contact us using the phone or address below, and we will permanently delete any related communication.

🌐 Third-Party Links & External Resources

Occasionally, our cream encyclopedia or recipe pages may reference external sources (e.g., historical cream techniques, academic dairy research) via hyperlinks. Azure Plate Diary is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked websites. However, we only link to trustworthy, educational, or public-domain resources — never to commercial tracking-heavy entities.

⚖️ Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Global Privacy)

Even though we do not store personal data, we respect and acknowledge your privacy rights:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request any personal information we may hold. Given our no-storage policy, there will be no data to report.
  • Right to Deletion: Since we do not retain records, there is nothing to delete. If you previously sent an email manually to us, you can request its erasure by reaching out.
  • Right to Object / Restrict Processing: We do no processing beyond responding to direct inquiries. You can opt out of any future communication by simply asking us.
  • Right to Data Portability: Not applicable as no automated processing occurs.

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have privacy concerns, please use the contact details listed at the bottom of this page. We will respond within 15 business days.

🍰 Cookies & Similar Technologies — None Exist

We repeat: Azure Plate Diary does not use cookies, localStorage tracking, sessionStorage identifiers, fingerprinting, or any browser storage mechanism to identify returning visitors. Our site is entirely static HTML/CSS/JS with no backend session management. The only JavaScript used is for smooth scrolling, dynamic content toggles (recipe details), and simulated form feedback — none of which stores or transmits any personal data. If your browser asks about cookie permissions, rest assured we never set them.

🍪 "Cream cookies" in our kitchen refer to shortbread biscuits — we never bake digital trackers. Your privacy remains untouched like a fresh snow of powdered sugar.
