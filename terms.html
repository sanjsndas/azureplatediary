📜 Terms of Privacy & Legal Framework

Effective date: January 1, 2025 | Last updated: March 15, 2025

Welcome to Azure Plate Diary (the "Website", "we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is not merely respected – it is structurally protected. This document details our binding commitments, your rights, and the absolute absence of data harvesting. As a cream-centric culinary archive, we believe that trust, like a perfect crème anglaise, requires transparency and gentle care. Please read these Terms of Privacy carefully; by accessing or using azureplatediary.com (or viewing any of our static pages), you acknowledge and agree to the practices described herein.

1. Zero Data Collection – The Core Principle

Azure Plate Diary operates as a 100% tracker-free, cookie-free, analytics-free sanctuary. We do not:

  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, or browsing behavior.
  • Use any third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixels, or similar).
  • Embed tracking scripts, beacons, fingerprinting mechanisms, or session replay software.
  • Store any persistent identifiers or unique cookies on your device.
  • Share, sell, or license any user data because we simply possess none.

This website is entirely static HTML/CSS/JavaScript hosted on privacy-respecting infrastructure. No server-side logging of personal data occurs beyond essential ephemeral request data (which is automatically purged within hours and never analyzed). We do not create user profiles. You may browse all cream recipes, articles, and stories without any digital shadow. Your love for double cream ganache remains your own secret.

⚖️ Legal clarity regarding IP & logs: Our hosting provider’s standard server logs may temporarily retain anonymized technical data (e.g., request timestamps, bytes transferred) for security and uptime monitoring. Such data does not include IP addresses in any identifiable form after 24 hours and is never accessed by Azure Plate Diary staff for marketing or tracking. We have explicitly disabled all optional logging features.

2. Cookies – None, Ever

This Website does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, or any browser storage mechanism for identification, preferences, or analytics. There are no "accept cookies" pop-ups because there are no cookies to accept. Even functional or strictly necessary cookies (like login tokens) are irrelevant since we have no accounts, memberships, or comment sections. You will never find a tracking pixel or an advertising tag. Our pages are as clean as a freshly whipped double cream.

For further details, please refer to our dedicated Cookies Policy (which essentially repeats: no cookies).

3. No Third-Party Services, No Embedded Trackers

Unlike most websites, Azure Plate Diary does not embed fonts from Google, videos from YouTube, maps, social share buttons, or any external content that could potentially set cookies or collect data. All assets (CSS, minimal JS) are self-hosted or locally inlined. We do not use CDNs that could introduce tracking. We never integrate advertising networks, affiliate link trackers, or remarketing tools. Even our contact information is plain text (phone and address) — we don't use contact forms that might store messages. Should you choose to call or write physically, that communication remains strictly offline and confidential.

4. Children’s Privacy & General Audience

Our content is intended for general audiences, including young aspiring cooks interested in culinary arts. However, we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, regardless of age. Because we collect no data, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations are effortlessly satisfied; there is no personal information to delete or verify. Parents and guardians can rest assured that exploring cream sauces, custards, and pastry techniques involves zero data risk.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR, CCPA & Global Frameworks

Even though we do not handle personal data, we fully endorse and respect user privacy rights:

  • Right to Access / Rectification / Erasure: Since we hold no data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Nevertheless, you may contact us via phone or physical mail (footer) with any inquiry, and we will provide written confirmation of our data-free operations.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We never sell data, so no opt-out needed. You are automatically opted-out by design.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Exercising privacy rights will never affect your experience because we don't differentiate users.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: In the unlikely event you believe your privacy has been impacted, please reach out, and we will transparently address your concerns.

For residents of the European Economic Area (EEA), California (USA), Brazil (LGPD), or other regions — you enjoy maximum privacy protections by default on our site.

If you leave our site via a link, we encourage you to review the privacy policies of those destinations. We also deliberately avoid using trackable outbound links (no UTM parameters or redirectors). Even the absence of data deserves a fortress-like approach.
